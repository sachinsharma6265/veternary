Explore Our Membership

Memebership

The Ghana Veterinary Medicine Association was founded in 2005 to advocate for veterinary professionals in Ghana. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to promote animal wemfare and enhance the professional development or our members.

2023 years

Since our inception we have grown into a leading voice for veterinary medicine in Ghana ensuring the highest standards of animal health and welfare.

100 members

Our association is committed integrity collaboration and excellence in veterinary practice striving to support our members in every way possible

20 initiatives

We actively engage in various initiatives to enhance the skills and knowledge of veterinary professionals ensuring they are equipped with the latest advancements in veterinary medicine

15 workshops

Our workshop and training programs are designed to foster professional grouth and development helping our members stay informed and engaged in the field.

Students

Our student programs and workshops are tailored to enhance your learning experience and personal development. We offer valuable opportunities to build essential skills, stay informed on the latest industry trends, and connect with peers, all aimed at preparing you for academic success and future career opportunities.

Clinics

Our clinic services are designed to provide exceptional care and support to our patients. With a focus on personalized treatment, advanced medical practices, and compassionate healthcare, we aim to promote wellness and improve the quality of life for every individual we serve
